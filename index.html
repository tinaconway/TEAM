About Us

Baltimore, a place with a heavy shadow over it these days, seems like an unlikely place for a group like this to form. A white kid born and raised on a farm worshipping guitar players crosses paths with two black rappers from the city and the combination brings about a fresh new sound, "Trapedelia". Drawing Inspiration from groups like Pink Floyd, Outkast, NWA, Led Zeppelin and many others enables TEAM to create a sonic palette for anyones taste. Beats that are emotionally engaging hand in hand with lyricism and content that paint the perfect picture for the audience are what makes TEAM special. Baltimore has been without a sound for too long. It's time that the world sees that there is more to Charm City than just riots and dismay. That there is beautiful art here that deserves a chance to be seen and heard. Out of the ashes of Baltimore came T.E.A.M.
